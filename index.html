Nikola Tesla

Contributions to Science

  • Designed the alternating-current (AC) electrical system
  • Designed what was among the first AC hydroelectric power plants in the United States, at Niagara Falls.
  • Patented the Tesla coil, which laid the foundation for wireless technologies and is still used in radio technology today.
  • Pioneer in the discovery of radar technology, X-ray technology, remote control and the rotating magnetic field — the basis of most AC machinery.

“The scientists of today think deeply instead of clearly. One must be sane to think clearly, but one can think deeply and be quite insane.”

-- Nikola Tesla
